# Hi, I’m Veasna 👋

### AI-Assisted Builder · UI/UX Designer · Technology & Infrastructure Explorer

I build practical digital systems by combining **AI, product thinking, UI/UX design, software, networking, and hardware**.

I may not remember every programming syntax from memory—and I no longer measure my ability that way. My strength is knowing how to use tools such as **Claude, ChatGPT, and Obsidian** to research problems, plan solutions, design interfaces, generate and review code, troubleshoot systems, and turn ideas into working products.

I enjoy working across the full problem—not only the code.

## 🚀 What I Work On

* Building and improving POS and business-management systems
* Designing operational workflows and user interfaces
* Using AI agents to develop, debug, document, and coordinate projects
* Managing Raspberry Pi devices across different networks
* Configuring Linux systems, background services, VPNs, and remote access
* Connecting software with barcode scanners, printers, touchscreens, and other hardware
* Translating real business requirements into product specifications and system architecture

## 🧠 Current Projects and Interests

* **KitLuy Suite** — POS systems for laundry and café businesses
* **HSA** — laundry pickup, delivery, and operations ecosystem
* **SroulApp** — Cambodian messaging and super-app product design
* **Raspberry Pi Management Platform** — monitoring and remotely managing branch devices
* **AI-Assisted Development** — improving how humans and AI agents build products together
* **Infrastructure and Networking** — Linux, Tailscale, remote desktop, device communication, and offline-first systems

## 🤖 How I Use AI

I use AI as a technical partner throughout the development process:

* Researching technologies and possible solutions
* Converting business ideas into clear requirements
* Planning architectures and implementation steps
* Generating and reviewing code
* Debugging errors and infrastructure problems
* Creating UI concepts, prototypes, and design systems
* Writing technical documentation and handoff guides
* Coordinating large projects across multiple AI sessions

My goal is not to let AI make every decision. My goal is to provide the context, direction, testing, and judgment needed to make AI useful.
